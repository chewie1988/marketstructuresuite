The Process

A Systematic Approach to Every Trade

Each indicator narrows the field. Used together in order they give you complete institutional context before you enter a single trade.

01

Establish Bias

Check the VWAP Suite. Price above dyVWAP = bullish. Below = bearish. This is your global direction.

→
02

Find Your Zone

Check Smart Money Range. Only look for longs in Discount. Only shorts in Premium. Wrong zone = no trade.

→
03

Identify the Level

Check OB Scanner and FVG Scanner. Find a graded order block or FVG in your zone. This is your entry and stop loss.

04

Confirm with Volume
