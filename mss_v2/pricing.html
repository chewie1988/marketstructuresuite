Frequently Asked Questions

Do the indicators repaint?

No. All calculations are based on confirmed candle closes. What you see on the chart will not change after the candle closes.

What markets do they work on?

All indicators work on any liquid market with OHLCV data — crypto, stocks, forex, commodities. Optimised parameters are based on crypto 4H testing.

What TradingView plan do I need?

Any paid TradingView plan (Essential or above) supports invite-only indicators. Free accounts cannot use invite-only scripts.

Is there a refund policy?

Given the digital nature of indicator access, refunds are not available after access has been granted. If you have questions before purchasing, please reach out first.
