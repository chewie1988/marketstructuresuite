🔲 Entry Identification

Institutional OB Scanner

Order blocks are where institutions placed their orders. When price returns to these zones those orders are still active — providing real support and resistance, not arbitrary lines. The OB Scanner finds them automatically and grades their quality so you always know the conviction behind the level.

Automatic bullish and bearish order block detection on confirmed closes

Three-tier grading — A++ (FVG nested + 3%+ impulse + 1.5x volume), A+ (2%+ impulse), A (standard)

Adjustable lookback — default 3 candles, optimised through 8 years of backtesting

Boxes end at OB candle — no misleading forward projection

Built-in stop loss logic — use OB if within 2%, override at 1.5% if 2-4%, reject above 4%

No repainting — all calculations on confirmed bar closes

⚡ Imbalance Detection

Smart Money FVG Scanner

Fair value gaps are price inefficiencies institutions leave behind when they move markets aggressively. These gaps act as magnets — price almost always returns to fill them. The FVG Scanner finds them automatically and highlights the rare Unicorn setup when a gap forms at a swing extreme.

Automatic bullish and bearish FVG detection with minimum gap size filter

Clean impulse candle filter — only marks FVGs with genuine directional momentum

Recent FVGs (default 3) — fully extended, high visibility, actively monitored

Historical FVGs (default 5) — faded reference levels for context

50% midline on every FVG — the critical retest level within the gap

Unicorn detection (purple) — FVG at swing extreme, the highest conviction setup

Automatic mitigation — gaps disappear when price fully closes through them

Three alert conditions — bullish FVG, bearish FVG, Unicorn setup
